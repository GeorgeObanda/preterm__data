Preterm Africa Study

The Preterm Africa Study is dedicated to improving the health and wellbeing of mothers and babies across Africa by understanding preterm births, their causes, and outcomes.

Our goal is to support research, raise awareness, and provide valuable resources to communities, healthcare workers, and policymakers. Together, we aim to reduce the incidence and impact of preterm births through collaboration and innovation.
